About Me

I am a digital media specialist with more than ten years of media experience. I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Pretoria and Stellenbosch University. Between 2008 and 2009 I worked in news media as a junior journalist and tech reporter. I then did my masters at Indiana University. I returned to South Africa in 2012 and since then I have worked in the agency space and as a digital strategist and data journalist with the likes of Trigger Isobar, Code for South Africa and the South African Broadcasting Corporation. Currently I am the program officer at the South Africa Media Innovation Program and one of the executive committee members of Hacks/Hackers Johannesburg. I am fascinated by the evolution of media from the way we consume content to how we interact on social media. I am also an aspiring data analyst with numerous certifications and projects.
