About Me


I am the program officer for the South Africa Media Innovation Program. Innovation is something that has been an interest of mine since I began my media career at the University of Pretoria.


I studied journalism at Stellenbosch University after my undergraduate degree in International Relations at Tuks. I then went on to study new media at Indiana University where I experimented in different forms of interactive storytelling as well the business of media.


You can call me a digital fundie - I love to learn and I love to try out new things in this space. Currently I am dabbling in data driven storytelling and projects.


Check out my LinkedIn profile and Github page for more information on me.
